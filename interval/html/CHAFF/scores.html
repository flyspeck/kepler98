Anomaly 1. Four anchored simplices and one large gap

Group

  • The four anchored simplices
  • Any specials along the anchors
  • The subcluster at the large gap P

Scoring

  • The upright quarters score sigma0
  • The other anchored simplices use vor or vor0
  • The subcluster is scored with a knob at each vertex of P that is not a vertex of one of the anchored simplices.

Bounds

  • tau > 7.95 pt + (n-5) 1.53 pt - 0.008 [n-4/3]
  • sigma < ?

Notes

  1. There is at most one special along an anchored simplex.
    1.65+2(1.41)+2(0.956)>pi
  2. The subcluster P is not a simplex, because the gap is large.
  3. The group has at least 6 external edges.

Anomaly 2. Three anchors concentrated in a half plane

Scoring

  • The upright quarters score sigma0
  • The subcluster is scored with a knob at each vertex of P that is not a vertex of one of the anchored simplices and that is not dependent.

  • tau > 10.12 pt + (n-5) 1.53 pt - 0.008 [n-4/3]
  • sigma < ?

Notes

  1. The three anchored simplices are quarters.
    1.41+2(0.956)>pi
  2. The subcluster P is not a simplex.
  3. There is at most one dependent vertex.
  4. There are at least n-5 knobs that are used in the scoring.
